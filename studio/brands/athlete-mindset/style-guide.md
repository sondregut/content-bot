# Athlete Mindset — Brand Style Guide

## Brand Identity

- **App Name**: Athlete Mindset
- **Website**: athletemindset.app
- **Tagline**: "Your AI mental performance coach"
- **Value Prop**: Science-backed visualization, breathwork, and AI voice coaching — a sports psychologist in your pocket
- **Target Audience**: Athletes at all levels seeking mental performance training — high school, college, amateur, and competitive athletes
- **Mascot**: Owl (wisdom, calm focus)

## Color Palette

| Name   | Hex       | Usage                                |
|--------|-----------|--------------------------------------|
| Navy   | `#072F57` | Primary background, headers          |
| Cyan   | `#73A6D1` | Highlights, accents, links           |
| White  | `#FFFFFF` | Primary text on dark backgrounds     |
| Beige  | `#D9D0C2` | Warm alternate backgrounds           |
| Green  | `#43AA32` | CTA buttons ONLY — never decoration  |

## Typography

- Headlines: Modern sans-serif (Inter / SF Pro), extra-bold, tight line-height
- Body: Regular weight, comfortable line-height
- Clean kerning, high readability

## Content Pillars

1. **Visualization Science** — research showing mental rehearsal improves performance
2. **Mental Training as Competitive Edge** — the mindset separates good from great
3. **Accessibility** — AI mental performance coach at a fraction of a sports psychologist's cost ($200/hr)
4. **CTA** — "Try free" / "athletemindset.app" / download nudge

## Slide Design Rules

### Text Slides
- Dark premium navy/near-black background with subtle grain
- Large left-aligned text within safe zones (top 180px, bottom 320px, sides 90px)
- Plenty of negative space
- Headline: extra-bold, tight line-height
- Body: regular weight, comfortable line-height
- Highlight phrases in Cyan `#73A6D1`

### Photo Slides
- Candid, authentic sports photography — iPhone-shot aesthetic
- Natural lighting, shallow depth of field, 35mm film grain
- No staged or glamorized scenes
- Overlay: dark gradient or frosted glass for text readability
- Subject: real skin texture, natural proportions, authentic worn gear
- Lighting: soft natural daylight, no dramatic studio lighting

### CTA Slides
- Green `#43AA32` button or accent
- "Try free today" / "athletemindset.app"
- Clean and uncluttered

## Tone & Voice

- Empathetic and motivational
- Science-backed — always cite research
- Accessible — not overly academic
- Encouraging — "you can train your mind like the pros"

## App Icon Overlay

- Position: bottom-right (default), 8% of image width
- Rounded iOS-style corners
- Text label: "athletemindset.app" below icon
- Opacity: 0.18 (subtle watermark)

## Tricky Words (always spell-check)

- "ATHLETE MINDSET" (two words, all caps for micro-labels)
- "visualization" (not "visualisation")
- "athletemindset.app" (no space, no caps)
- "rehearsal"
- "psychologist"
- "Neuropsychologia"
